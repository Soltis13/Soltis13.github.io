Welcome to my Bootcamp School Assignment Website.

Hello, my name is Scott Soltis and this webpage covers my efforts in the University of Minnesota Bootcamp Program.

This program is a 6 month program coving full stack technologies. (HTMLCSS and Javascript to NodeJS, Express, and Mysql). Prioir to joining this program I worked as an Quality and R&D Engineer in the desing and control of medical device technology and its manufacturing.

My passion is around providing a great user-to-technology expereince. Wether that is designing high end equipment with user eurganomics or communicating with customers, educating and troobleshooting existing product, I've always strived to understand and improve technology for the better. I've found that technology is great for improveing human experiences but only if it is designed to be invisable and complimentary to the situation.

Web Development and computer programming is another avenue of that user deisgn experience. With the internet acting as the identiy of that 21st century human experience, there is no other technology development oportunty like it.
