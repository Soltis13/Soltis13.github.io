What is the difference between a framework, toolkit and library?
    Inversion of Control - When you call a library, you are in control.  But with a framework
    , the control is inverted, the framework calls you.
    In the framework, the control flow is based on predefened spots to fill out with code.  

    A kit is a set of predefened libraries that have been setup and tested to work together.  
    A sort of larger meta library.
